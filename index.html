About Me


I received my Bachelor's in Music from Carnegie Mellon University and my MFA from the Berklee College of Music. I've done everything from warming up the stage for artists like Willie Nelson, Van Morrison, Danilo Perez, and Jack White to going incognito at a local Christmas party to win a sing-off. Recently, I started coding as a way to use my creative energy and language skills. I’ve also made it my life’s mission to find the greatest mozzarella sticks in the Lower 48 while on tour with my band, The Commonheart.
